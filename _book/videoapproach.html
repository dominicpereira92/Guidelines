1.2 Video Approach

Video First Approach

Record the Videos

Each video will be within 5 to 8 minutes and will cover 1 topic within the section.

Get the videos reviewed

Our technical reviewers will review the screencasts and provide feedback.

Polish the Videos

Address the reviwer comments and polish the content in your videos.

  • Involves video recording at the initial stage.

  • Single stage finalization.

  • Pre-planned content.

  • Recommended if you are an expert at recording video or screencasts.

  • Does not facilitate easy revisions.
